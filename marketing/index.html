Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

🔒

100% Private

Your data never leaves your machine. No cloud sync, no accounts, no tracking. JSON files stored where you choose.

👥

People-Centric

Organize notes by person. Track roles, teams, and build a history of every conversation over time.

🏷️

Tag & Filter

Tag notes with topics like "hiring", "architecture", or "follow-up". Filter instantly to find what matters.

💡

Discussion Prompts

Never run out of things to ask. Pre-configured questions are suggested when you start a new note.

⚡

Instant & Lightweight

Launches in under a second. No Electron bloat — lean, fast, and focused on one thing: your meetings.

📂

Portable Data

Choose where your data lives. Move it to a USB drive, a synced folder, or keep it in the default location.

How it works

Simple by design

1

Add your people

Add the people you meet with regularly — their name, role, and team. Build your sidebar in seconds.

2

Take notes during your 1:1

Click a person, hit "New note", and start writing. A random discussion question is suggested to get the conversation flowing.

3

Review & prepare

Before your next meeting, scroll through past notes to remember context, follow-ups, and commitments. Filter by tag to focus on specific themes.

Built with

⚛️ Electron
🚂 Express.js
📄 Vanilla JS
